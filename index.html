READ BEFORE ENGAGING

General note—disclaimer.

All my fics are written in a limited point of view. That means you're in the characters' heads—their thoughts, their feelings, their perceptions. It is not omniscient. What they think is what you read. That includes incorrect assumptions, emotional spirals, missing context, misinformation, internal contradictions, projections, and deeply flawed logic. Just like real people.

The narration is unreliable on purpose. Psychological realism is the foundation of everything I write. You're not getting the full truth—you're getting the character's truth. And if that frustrates you, that's okay. But this space isn't built for neat, all-knowing storytelling. It's built to feel like real internal chaos.
